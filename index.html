About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Management Science and Engineering at the University of Waterloo, supervised by Prof. Qi-Ming He and Prof. Stanko Dimitrov. I completed my PhD in the same department under the supervision of Prof. Stanko Dimitrov and Prof. Michael Pavlin. My research focuses on applying robust optimization to understand climate change adaptation and utilizing machine learning techniques to solve queueing models.
